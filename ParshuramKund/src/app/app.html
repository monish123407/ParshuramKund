Parshuram Kund Mela

District Administration, Lohit

An annual holy pilgrimage festival held at the Brahmakund, Lohit River in Arunachal Pradesh. Famously known as the "Kumbh of the Northeast", welcoming lakhs of devotees for the holy dip on Makar Sankranti.

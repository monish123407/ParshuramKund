How To Reach

Spiritual Destination of Arunachal Pradesh

To enable the visitors to obtain easy entry, District Administration has also been making suitable provisions for the issue of Inner Line Permit for 30 days both at Dirak check gate and Sunpura check gate for visitors from outside. To reach Parshuram kund the visitors can travel from either Dibrugarh-Tinsukia via Rupai near Doomdooma to Dirak check gate where Inner Line Permit will be issued on the spot to them. Another route is through Sadiya, crossing the Bhupen Hazarika Bridge via Sunpura to Tezu, the District Headquarters where a big shopping fare is held in January every year. For safe and convenient journey of pilgrims, Lohit District Administration has been liaising with Tinsukia District Administration to maintain security along both routes.

Reach By Air

The nearest airport is Dibrugarh Airport, Assam. Visitors can hire taxis or buses from Dibrugarh to Tezu and Parshuram Kund.

Connecting to satellite routing (Train)...

Reach By Train

The nearest railway station from Parshuram Kund is located in Tinsukia at a distance of about 160 km. Once you have reached the railway station, you can hire a cab or bus to Parshuram Kund

Connecting to satellite routing (Road)...

Reach By Road

Parshuram Kund is connected by road through Tezu, Namsai and Dibrugarh with regular taxi and bus services.
